Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the FEU Grade Calculator Activator (the “App”) accesses and uses data when you run the activation process for your copy of the FEU Grade Calculator spreadsheet. The web frontend is hosted at https://feu.jareddo06.ovh.

1. What we access

The App uses Google OAuth 2.0 to request permission to access only the Google Sheets data you explicitly allow. The App requests these scopes:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets — read and write access to spreadsheets required to populate and configure your copy of the calculator.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.external_request — enables the frontend to call the Apps Script backend function that runs the activation.

2. How we use the data

When you authorize the App and provide a spreadsheet ID, the Apps Script backend will:

  • Open your specified spreadsheet copy;
  • Read the range reference you configured (for example Main!AB5) and read source rows from the Raw sheet;
  • Filter and write resulting rows into Main!B6:E15 (or write "N/A" when appropriate);
  • Unhide the Main and Raw sheets and delete the README sheet in your copy, as part of the activation flow;
  • Write a small activation flag into a cell (e.g. AA4) so the copy knows activation completed.

All actions occur only on the spreadsheet you specify and only after you explicitly authorize the App.

3. Do we store or share your data?

No. The App does not store your spreadsheet contents on external servers, nor does it sell or share your data with third parties. All reading and writing is performed within Google’s infrastructure via Apps Script on the spreadsheet you own or control.

4. How to revoke access

You can revoke the App’s access at any time from your Google Account permissions page:
https://myaccount.google.com/permissions

After revocation the App will no longer be able to access or modify your spreadsheet.

5. Third-party services

This App uses these third-party services:

  • Google Apps Script / Google Cloud Platform — executes the activation logic and accesses the spreadsheet via the scopes you grant. See Google’s privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts the static frontend at feu.jareddo06.ovh. See GitHub’s privacy statement: docs.github.com.

6. Security

The App leverages Google’s OAuth 2.0 mechanism and runs within Google Apps Script. Communications are encrypted via HTTPS. We do not transfer or host your spreadsheet data outside Google’s services.

7. Children

The App is intended for general academic use and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Policy periodically. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Please revisit this page to stay informed of any changes.
